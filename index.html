Sapphire Crystal Glass

MORE

Sapphire is very strong and scratch-resistant, making it the best choice for premium watches.
Sapphire is more expensive than acryli100c and mineral glass, but it has advantages due to scratch and scattering resistance.
In addition to anti-scratch features, sapphire watch glass can withstand cracks and breaks better than mineral or acrylic glass.

It is believed that only diamonds and other sapphires can scratch sapphires.
In the watch industry, it is common to use synthetic sapphires consisting of crystallized aluminum oxide.
Synthetic sapphires are colorless,but have the same physical properties as natural sapphires.
However, sapphire crystals require delicate work when milling and cutting.
